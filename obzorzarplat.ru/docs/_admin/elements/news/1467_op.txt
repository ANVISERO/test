According to the research of Mercer, average salary for the Chief’s of the largest USA companies (those one which are in Fortune 1000) is reduces on 15,8%. For CEO of companies with turnover more than 40 B $ average income was 14 M $. Chief’s of medium companies (with 14 B $ turnover and more) got somewhere about 9,4 M $. Financial compensation for the Chief’s of the companies with turnover 1,2 B $ and more was about 4,7 M $, and it is 4,6% below last years compensations.
